Make your Slack activity geo-relevant using nothing but your phone
Whether you are arriving to the #office, just came back #home, attending #that-conference or on-site with #a-particular-customer, SlackGo helps you stay in the right channels for your current location
SlackGo is really simple to use
Link your Slack account
Associate locations to channels
Join/Leave channels as you Enter/Exit locations
What can you do with SlackGo?
Have an #office channel?
Configure it so your team joins and leaves it as they enter/leave the office. Looking for someone who is at the office now becomes just issuing /who on the #office channel. Ideal for remote-friendly companies.
Are you with a team at a big, multi-location, CES-like event?
Those may get crazy and sometimes is hard to know who was where... create channels for the different locations and make sure you always reach the people that is at the right place.
Have a lot of interesting or funny channels on your organization, but don't want those to clutter your phone when you are out?
Set up a custom, smarter tune-off by leaving automatically all those channels as you leave your workplace.
